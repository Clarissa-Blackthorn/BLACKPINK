BLACKPINK IN YOUR AREA
The biggest Korean girl group in the world
Come witness the group in action and leave becoming a fan or as we like to say a BLINK
BLACKPINK debuted in October 2016 under YG Entertainment with four members Jisoo, Jennie, Rose and Lisa. Their first single album "Square One" featured songs "Boombayah" and "Whistle". Both songs entered Gaon Digital Charts as No 1 singles.
BLACKPINK has been taking the world by storm since their debut with chart topping number one singles both in Korea and internationally.They were the first K-Pop Girl group to perform at the Coachella 2019 and as a main act on opening day too.
Their studio album "The Album" became the first ever album by a Korean girl group to sell more then one million copies. BLACKPINK is the highest charting female Korean act on Billboard Hot 100 with "Ice Cream" peaking at number 13.
BLACKPINK became the first K-Pop group to have a music video reach one billion views on Youtube with "Ddu-Du Ddu-Du" in a little more than a year. They are also the first musical group and Korean act to have three music videos with one billion views each on Youtube.
